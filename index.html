Interests

I am interested in the linkage between culture and politics. In other words, I like to reflect upon the ways how everyday life attitudes and behaviors shape the polities we inhabit.
I research the production of statehood, collective memories, and the role authorized knowledge plays in defining and subverting political and social orders.
Finally, I am very interested in the field of digital humanities. It is an exciting way to build a public scholarship and enhance humanities' scholarship quality.
